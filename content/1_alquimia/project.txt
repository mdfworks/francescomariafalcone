Info: Francesco Maria Falcone (1994) is a graphic and motion designer originally from Melito Porto Salvo (RC). After his first steps as a designer in his native country, he specialized  in Art Direction and Graphic Design at the European Institute of Design (IED) in Rome and then he received a master's degree at Abadir Academy in Catania. After the studies, he began his career as a graphic motion designer in the capital, contributing to several visual identity projects. Currently, Francesco works in studio de Ronners (Rotterdam) and co-founded an Instagram page dedicated to graphic design and visual arts called Visualbleed.
